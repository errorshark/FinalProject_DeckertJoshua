FAQ

    1. I want to stream how would I best get started?
    Using your console is the cheapest and easiest way to start an audience and to develop your style of interaction.
     
    2. What should I stream to get followers?
    Any game that you really like to play, that people are interested in watching, because you are going to be playing it a lot.
     
    3. What is the best way to set up the stream?
    Styles are completely up to you, you can experiment with different ways that you have things set up and can pick what works best for you.
     
    4. How much does it cost to stream?
    As much as you want to invest into it. You can start out on a console and as your channel grows so can your equipment and amount of extras you bring to your unique channels.
     
    5. How long does it take to setup a working setup and stream?
    It can as little as a few minutes or you can be constantly changing and upgrading things as you go along until you are comfortable.
     
    6. Why should I use bots?
    Bots are going to be your best friends they help take of the heavy lifting reminding your audience of events and other ways to interact with you and the stream.
